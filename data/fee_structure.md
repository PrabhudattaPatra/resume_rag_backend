## Hostel Refund/Withdrawal/Booking Policy
| Sl.No | Fee Type | Refund Policy |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| 1 | Hostel – Room Rent | **Non-refundable**: Once enrollment is complete and classes commence, room rent for the academic year is non-refundable, even if the student does not use hostel premises. |
| 2 | Hostel – Mess Fee | **Refundable** after deducting: (a) Proportionate amount for months mess was utilized, (b) Maintenance charge of ₹5,000. Any outstanding university dues will be adjusted first; balance will be refunded. |
| 3 | Withdrawal of Hostel Admission | If student withdraws before classes start: ₹10,000 non-refundable deduction; remaining amount refunded. |
| 4 | New Admission Payment | 50% of hostel fee (Room Rent + Mess) must be deposited in advance. Remaining 50% due before reporting to hostel. |

## Important Notes
1. All fees are subject to change as per university notification.
2. Fees mentioned are tuition fees only; additional charges may apply for exams, library, lab, etc.
3. International student fees are in USD; payment can be made via bank transfer or approved payment gateway.
4. Scholarship-eligible students should apply separately; fees are payable in full until scholarship is disbursed.
5. For latest fee updates, visit: https://cgu-odisha.ac.in/fees-scholarship/
