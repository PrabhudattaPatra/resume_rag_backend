## Q1: About C.V. Raman Global University (CGU)
**Answer:**  
C.V. Raman Global University (CGU), located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, is a premier private institution dedicated to providing state-of-the-art education and fostering innovation.  
- Established in 1997 as C.V. Raman College of Engineering; elevated to university status in 2020 under the Odisha Act 01 of 2020.  
- Campus: 100+ acres, 8000+ students, 300+ international students from 22+ countries, 8+ international academic/research collaborations.  
- Research Output: 2236+ publications, 500+ books, 50+ government-funded projects.  

**Rankings & Accreditations:**  
- NIRF 2024: Top 100 Engineering Institutions in India (consistent since 2016)  
- NBA Accreditation: CSE, Mechanical Engineering, ECE  
- Recognized as S.I.R.O by D.S.I.R., Government of India  
- Times Engineering Survey 2024:  
  • 4th Best Engineering Institute in East Zone  
  • 9th Top Private Engineering Institute (All India)  
  • 9th for Placement among Private Institutions  
  • 9th in Research Capability  
  • 11th Overall Engineering Institute Ranking

## Q11: How do I apply for CGET-2025 at CGU and what should I keep in mind?
**Answer:**  
**Application Process:**  
- Apply online only via www.cgu-odisha.ac.in  
- No application or examination fee  
- Fill authentic and complete details only  
- Upload recent photograph and full signature  
- Application Number generated after submission  
- No hard copy required; retain printout for reference  

**Important Instructions:**  
- Only one application per candidate per stream  
- Multiple applications will be rejected  
- No changes allowed after submission  
- Incomplete/incorrect applications rejected  
- Admission cancelled if documents are fake/manipulated  
- Complete application within prescribed deadline  

**Reservation Categories:**  
| Category | Percentage of Seats |  
|----------|---------------------|  
| Scheduled Caste (SC) | 8% |  
| Scheduled Tribe (ST) | 12% |  
| Green Card (GC) | 5% |  
| Physically Challenged (PWD/PC) | 5% |  
| Ex-Serviceman (EX) | 3% |  

*Note: Proof not required during online registration; must provide valid documents during counselling/verification.*  

- Confirmation sent via email; provide correct email/contact  
- E-Admit card generated in student LOGIN portal  
- E-Admit card shows Roll Number, Photograph, Test Date/Time; report discrepancies to CHAIRMAN, CGET immediately

## Q37: Essential Academic Eligibility for Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech, B.Sc (Hons.) Agriculture, BBA) for International Students
**Answer:**  
Applicant should have ≥55% aggregate or 5.5 CGPA (10-point scale) in Senior Secondary [10+2] or equivalent recognized by AIU with:  
1. Physics and Mathematics compulsory + one of Chemistry/Biotechnology/Computer Science/Biology for B.Tech  
2. English compulsory for BBA  

**Additional Requirements (UG):**  
1. Valid TOEFL/IELTS score (waived if medium of instruction was English, certified by Head of Institution/Govt. Official)  
2. Merit list based on aggregate score in Physics, Mathematics, and core subjects  
3. Any other requirement prescribed by University  

---

## Q38: Essential Academic Eligibility for Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech, M.Sc, MCA, MBA) for International Students
**Answer:**  
Applicant should have ≥55% aggregate or 5.5 CGPA (10-point scale) in qualifying degree. Must have passed:  
- 4/5-year UG in Engineering/Technology or equivalent for M.Tech  
- Appropriate UG degree for MBA/M.Sc from education stream recognized by AIU/UGC/AICTE  

**Additional Requirements (PG):**  
1. Valid TOEFL/IELTS score (waived if medium of instruction was English)  
2. Admission based on aggregate score in qualifying degree

## Q40: General Eligibility Criteria for International Students
**Answer:**  
1. Degrees/Certificates must be recognized/approved by AIU as equivalent to Indian degrees  
2. Must hold valid passport from respective country  
3. Certified English transcripts required (non-English transcripts not acceptable)  
4. If grades awarded:  
   a. Submit conversion certificate from Institution/University if grade sheet doesn't show equivalent percentage  
   b. If grade sheet shows conversion formula/percentage, enclose photocopy showing both sides  

**Notes:**  
1. Cannot pursue another full-time course simultaneously at this or any other University/Institution  
2. Cannot change course after admission  
3. Must produce medical fitness certificate; HIV test mandatory (no admission if positive); required tests:  
   - HIV Test (AIDS Test)  
   - Chest check-up (X-Ray)  
   - Hepatitis Surface Antigen (HBsAg)  
   - General Medical Test  
4. Must obtain medical insurance cover for entire course duration (renewable yearly)
